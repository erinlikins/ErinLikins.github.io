Introduction

Hi, I'm Erin! Welcome to my Data Analyst Portfolio! I'm thrilled to share my journey as a data analyst, showcasing my expertise in data cleaning, data analysis, data visualization, and problem-solving using a combination of Excel, R, SQL, and Tableau. Throughout this portfolio, you'll find a collection of projects that highlight my skills in extracting insights from complex datasets, transforming raw data into actionable information, and creating compelling visualizations to tell data-driven stories.

My passion for data analysis began while I was working as a student researcher in an air quality lab at the University of Utah. Since then, I have used data in a variety of settings to find solutions to the real-world problems in my own life. These projects ranged from analyzing sales data from my online clothing resale business to discover trends and increase my return on investment, to analyzing my personal health and fitness data to achieve certain workout goals.

Feel free to explore the projects, visualizations, and analyses I've prepared. If you have any questions, feedback, or would like to collaborate, please don't hesitate to reach out. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy your journey through my data analyst portfolio!
